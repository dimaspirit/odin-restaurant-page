Baba's Kitchen Drogheda

About

Nestled in the heart of Drogheda, Baba's Kitchen brings authentic Ukrainian flavors to Ireland. Our family-run restaurant celebrates generations of traditional recipes passed down from our beloved grandmothers (babas), combined with a warm, modern atmosphere. Experience the soulful taste of Ukraine through our handmade varenyky, hearty borscht, and signature Chicken Kyiv.
